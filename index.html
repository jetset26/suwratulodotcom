Born and raised in Washington DC (like actual DC, not Rockville or Arlington), I grew up in the 90s surrounded by the things that made that decade as great as it was: new cartoons, playstation, the diversity of the city, POKEMON! As the 90s launch led into the 2000s take-off, I became enamoured with art and music and technology. My gift of drawing my favorite cartoons and video game characters led me to thinking of graphic design. My familiarity of computers that came from spending time playing video games with friends led me to thinking of pursuing the technology field. Then Steve Jobs did us all a solid and ushered in this new era of web graphics and mobile apps and, though I was slow to respond, my response was still a, "Let's do this."
